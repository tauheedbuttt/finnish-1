📍 Local Cases Overview

Missä? Where (static)
Mistä? From where
Mihin? To where

Internal (S-cases) = inside places | External (L-cases) = outside/surface

➡️ Mihin (To) - Internal Rules

-Vn Stem ends with 1 vowel → add 'n' talo → taloon, Suomi → Suomeen
-seen Stem ends with 2 identical vowels huone → huoneeseen
-hVn Short words (1 syllable) with 2 vowels maa → maahan, työ → työhön
-hin Weekdays ending with 'tai' maanantai → maanantaihin
